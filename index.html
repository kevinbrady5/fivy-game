How To Play

The goal of NewGame is to get rid of all your cards.

  • NewGame is played in rounds
  • Each round has a random round goal
  • You get rid of cards by playing them when it is your turn
  • You can play the round goal by laying it down in front of you or you can play additional cards on another player's layed down cards
  • These additional cards must follow the round goal
  • Each round is made up a set number of turns
  • Each turn has a random turn modifier
  • The turn modifier will give instructions that all players follow before the turn starts or it will specify a modifier that lasts for that turn only
  • After the last turn, the round is finished

Here is an example of gameplay:

  1. This first...
  2. Then this.
  3. Now do this?
  4. Finish game!
